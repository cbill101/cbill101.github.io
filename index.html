About Me

Journey

I'm a budding software engineer born and raised in Utah. I started getting into programming in high school and loved it since. I first started off writing simple Python scripts, then transitioned into C# and Java through Granite Technical Institute, my high school district's skills campus.

Once into college, I knew I made the right decision. I started to learn the basics and fundamentals of software engineering and began to really appreciate what they do. I learned the most during senior year, while juggling two sizable projects being Canvas Polls and SKi Lift. Canvas was the first time I dove into a production codebase, modified it with a feature, and shown it to others, while working in a 4 man team. I learned how to be a leader and how to go about managing a team thanks to both those projects.
